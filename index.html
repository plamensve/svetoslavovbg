Services

Wholesale trade

GTA Petroleum trades petroleum products on a wholesale basis. The company conducts its commercial activities in Bulgaria, Romania, Serbia, Kosovo, and Greece.

Unloading of rail tank cars

To facilitate operations, we have a railway unloading facility for receiving petroleum products by rail tank cars. The base also accepts fuel deliveries by road tankers.

Loading of road tankers

The GTA Petroleum oil depot in Byala has two loading terminals for dispatching petroleum products and bio-components by road tankers.

Storage for up to 30 days

We provide tank storage capacity for operational purposes to interested parties, with the service including unloading, blending, and loading.

Storage under the Law on Oil and Petroleum Products

We provide tank storage capacity for diesel fuel in accordance with the Law on Oil and Petroleum Products Reserves (ZNNN) to obligated parties.

Licensed activities

We offer storage tanks for diesel fuel to companies importing petroleum products, as well as to those obligated under the Law on Oil and Petroleum Products Reserves (ZNNN) and the Excise Duties and Tax Warehouses Act (EDTWA).

Blending with bio-component

We have an automated in-line blending system for loading road tankers with mineral diesel fuel and bio-component (FAME).

Marking for bunkering

We offer an automated system for marking mineral diesel fuel with the blue euro marker GOM BLUE BU 10 Y for the bunkering of waterborne vessels.
